Find Our Clinic

Visit our modern dental clinic at Total Energies Petrol Station in Ruaraka

Exact Location Details

Building: Total Energies Petrol Station, Ruaraka

Landmark: Directly opposite Safari Park Hotel

Road: Thika Road (main highway)

Floor: 1st Floor, Room F14

Parking & Access

Ample parking available at the petrol station. Use the main entrance and take the stairs or elevator to the 1st floor.

Public Transport

Matatus to Ruaraka, Githurai, Kahawa West. Alight at Safari Park Hotel bus stop. Cross to Total Energies.

How to Find Us

By Car

Drive along Thika Road towards Ruaraka. Look for Safari Park Hotel on your left. We're directly opposite in the Total Energies building.

By Matatu

Take any matatu going to Ruaraka, Githurai, or Kahawa West. Alight at Safari Park Hotel bus stop. Cross the road to Total Energies.

On Foot

From Safari Park Hotel main gate, use the footbridge to cross Thika Road. Enter Total Energies compound and go to 1st floor, Room F14.

By Taxi/Uber

Set destination to "Total Energies, Ruaraka, opposite Safari Park Hotel". The clinic is on the 1st floor of the petrol station building.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our clinic

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card to your appointment.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for major treatments. Discuss options during your consultation.

How do I prepare for my first visit?

Bring your ID, insurance card, list of medications, and any previous dental records.
